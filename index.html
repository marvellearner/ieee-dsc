About The Congress

IEEE Delhi Section brings one of its biggest annual events, Delhi Section Congress 2017. Attracting over 300 delegates from more than 50-60 Student Branches, DSC is a great opportunity to reach out and engage students from every institution and the field of Engineering. The Congress was launched with the two primary objectives. The first was to provides a platform where students can collaborate and learn from our industry partners, sponsors and seniors and the second was to facilitate interaction and coordination between different student branches across the section enhancing networking.

The event provides attendees with tangible, real-life examples and help them improve their interpersonal and professional skills.

Delhi Section Congress is a magnificent opportunity, an edifying experience and invaluable platform for the delegates to ideate and collaborate.

About IEEE

IEEE is a non-profit corporation, incorporated in the state of New York on 16 March, 1896.The purpose of the IEEE is to engage exclusively in scientific and educational activities directed towards the advancement of the theory and practice of electrical, electronics, communications and computer engineering, as well as computer science, the allied branches of engineering and the related arts and sciences. IEEE and its members inspire a global community to innovate for a better tomorrow through its more than 423,000 members in over 160 countries. Official Website : www.ieee.org



About IEEE Council

IEEE India Council , which is currently Chaired by Dr. Sivaji Chakravorti, is the umbrella organisation which coordinates IEEE activities in India. It's primary aim is to assist and coordinate the activities of local “Sections”, in order to benefit mutually, and avoid duplication of effort and resources. IEEE India Council was established on 20th May 1976 and is one of the five councils in the Asia Pacific Region (Region #10 of IEEE),



About IEEE Delhi Section

IEEE Delhi Section is one of the vibrant Sections in India coming under Asia-Pacific Region, the Region 10 of IEEE. It covers entire northern part of the country consisting of the National Capital Territory of Delhi, Union Territory of Chandigarh and the five states of Rajasthan, Haryana, Punjab, Himachal Pradesh and Jammu & Kashmir. IEEE Delhi Section, which started on May 13, 1976 (after remaining as Sub-section since 1974), has as per January 2016 statistics (excluding Affiliates to Societies) 4699 active members including 2449 student members from over 80 member colleges, a number that is increasing with rapid pace by the day. IEEE Delhi follows standard IEEE and Section bylaws while operating. It organizes from time to time activities for dissemination of knowledge in the broader fields of Engineering through holding of technical talk, seminar, conference, symposium, workshop, tutorial, etc. Not only this, IEEE Delhi events also promote exchange of ideas and sharing of knowledge amongst the professionals in the corresponding fields. As a society, it has always focused on giving the student members services that justify the membership fee by opening new avenues and providing exciting, yet equal opportunities for one and all. With new students joining college and IEEE every year, the family is certain to grow. IEEE Delhi’s belief in imparting technical as well as non-technical knowledge will be carried forward with an equivalent enthusiasm and gusto for a long long time to come.
